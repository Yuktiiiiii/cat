# Meet Kutchu 🐾

## Welcome to Kutchu's World

Kutchu is a charming orange tabby cat from Bhopal with a big personality packed into a small, furry body. Known for his expressive eyes, playful nature, and endless curiosity, Kutchu has become a beloved companion and an important member of his family.

Whether he's exploring corners of the house, hiding in unexpected places, or following his favorite human around, Kutchu brings joy, laughter, and a little bit of mischief wherever he goes.

---

## About Kutchu

Kutchu is a handsome ginger tabby cat with beautiful golden eyes and a sleek striped coat. His alert ears, curious expression, and elegant posture make him impossible to ignore. Despite his adventurous appearance, Kutchu has a gentle and affectionate side that he reserves for the people he trusts.

### Quick Facts

* **Name:** Kutchu
* **Location:** Bhopal, India
* **Species:** Domestic Cat
* **Coat:** Orange/Ginger Tabby
* **Eye Color:** Golden Amber
* **Personality:** Curious, playful, cautious, affectionate

One of the most important relationships in Kutchu's life is with **Beeg**.
